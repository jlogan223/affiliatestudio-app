Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 April 2026

AffiliateStudio ("the tool", "we") is operated by PB Eng Ltd, a private company limited by shares and registered in England & Wales. The tool is used internally by PB Eng Ltd to manage its own Pinterest Business accounts. It is not made available to third parties.

1. Who this policy applies to

This privacy policy applies to the single operator of AffiliateStudio — a person acting on behalf of PB Eng Ltd — and to any personal data that the tool touches during the course of its operation.

AffiliateStudio is not offered as a product or service to any third party. It does not have external end users.

2. What data the tool processes

AffiliateStudio processes the following categories of information, all locally on the operator's own computer:

  • Pinterest OAuth tokens for the Pinterest Business accounts owned by PB Eng Ltd. These tokens are stored only on the operator's local machine and are used solely to authenticate API calls made by the operator against their own accounts.
  • Pin content drafted by the operator — images, titles, descriptions, destination links, board selections — held in a local data file under the operator's control.
  • Amazon product data (public product title, image URL, price at time of drafting) retrieved by the operator from public Amazon product pages.
  • Performance data returned by the Pinterest API for the operator's own pins (impressions, saves, outbound clicks), used in real time to inform the operator's next drafting session.

3. What the tool does not do

  • It does not collect data about any Pinterest user other than the operator's own accounts.
  • It does not combine Pinterest API data with any other service.
  • It does not share, sell, rent, license or otherwise disclose Pinterest data to any third party.
  • It does not cache or persist data returned by the Pinterest API beyond the immediate session needed to display it to the operator. Each time the tool needs Pinterest data, it calls the API fresh.
  • It does not use Pinterest data for any form of advertising, behavioural targeting, benchmarking, competitor research or off-platform analytics.
  • It does not scrape Pinterest or access accounts other than those for which the operator holds valid OAuth authorisation.
  • It does not transmit OAuth credentials or Pinterest data to any external server operated by the tool or its author.

4. Where data is stored

All data processed by AffiliateStudio is stored locally on the operator's own computer and on the operator's own private network storage. No data is uploaded to any server operated by PB Eng Ltd or by any third party other than:

  • Pinterest (for API calls the operator explicitly authorises)
  • Amazon (for publicly accessible product pages the operator visits in their own browser)
  • An email relay service used by the operator to send themselves notifications

5. How the tool complies with Pinterest's Developer Guidelines

AffiliateStudio is designed to align with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines. Specifically:

  • Every action taken on Pinterest is initiated by the operator, with the operator's specific knowledge and consent.
  • Every pin published is individually reviewed, edited where necessary, and explicitly approved by the operator before it goes to Pinterest.
  • No data received from the Pinterest API is stored longer than required to display it to the operator in real time.
  • The tool does not offer benchmarking, competitor research, or any form of platform insight beyond what Pinterest makes available to the operator for their own accounts.
  • The tool uses the operator's own API credentials ("bring-your-own-key" model) and keeps them on the operator's local machine.

6. Your rights (UK GDPR)

Because AffiliateStudio is a single-operator internal tool, the only person whose data is processed is the operator themselves, acting as both data controller and data subject on behalf of PB Eng Ltd. The operator retains all rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to access, correct, delete or port any personal data held in the tool. Questions about data handling can be sent to the contact address below.

This policy may be updated as the tool evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent change.
