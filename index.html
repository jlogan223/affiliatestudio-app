A Pinterest content studio for a single creator's own accounts.

AffiliateStudio is a personal content creation and scheduling tool used by PB Eng Ltd to manage its own Pinterest Business accounts. Every pin is designed, reviewed, edited and approved by a human before it is published.

What this is

AffiliateStudio is an internal tool built and used by PB Eng Ltd, a UK limited company, to help one person manage a small number of niche Pinterest Business accounts owned by the same company. It is not a multi-tenant service, not a marketplace, and not sold, rented or licensed to third parties.

The tool exists because designing a high-quality pin involves several steps — finding a product worth recommending, sourcing the right image, writing a clear title and description, choosing a suitable board, and composing the visual. AffiliateStudio brings those steps into one place so the creator can do them carefully rather than rushing.

How it works

1. The creator researches and drafts

The creator (or an assistant used by the creator) identifies products that fit one of the niches they cover, and uses AffiliateStudio to draft a proposed pin: a visual layout, a title, a description, relevant hashtags, and the destination link.

2. The creator reviews, edits and approves every pin

Nothing is published automatically. Every single pin is presented to the creator for review. The creator can:

  • Edit the pin's visual layout, colours, typography and composition
  • Rewrite the title, description, hook and alt text
  • Change the destination board
  • Add or remove hashtags and tagged topics
  • Reject the pin entirely and start over, or discard it

Only after the creator explicitly approves a pin does it move to the next step.

3. The creator schedules publishing

Approved pins sit in a queue. The creator chooses when each one is published — either by publishing it immediately, or by assigning it to a scheduled slot in line with the Pinterest API's published rate limits and the platform's community guidelines. The creator retains the ability to edit, reorder, hold or remove any queued pin before it is published.

4. The creator monitors performance

Once pins are live, the creator uses Pinterest's own analytics to see how each one performs and adjusts future drafts accordingly. AffiliateStudio does not combine Pinterest data with any other service or share it with any third party.

Who uses it

One person, at one UK limited company, managing that company's own Pinterest Business accounts. No external users, no client accounts, no re-selling.

Affiliate disclosure

Some pins published via AffiliateStudio include Amazon Associates affiliate links. These are clearly and prominently disclosed on every pin using #ad and #affiliate in the pin description and tagged topics, consistent with both the UK Competition and Markets Authority guidance on affiliate disclosure and Pinterest's Community Guidelines. Disclosures are never hidden in the title.

Data and privacy

AffiliateStudio runs locally on the creator's own computer. Pinterest OAuth credentials are stored only on that computer. Data accessed from the Pinterest API is requested on demand and not cached or re-used for any other purpose. See the Privacy Policy for full detail.
